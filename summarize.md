# Reusable Prompt for Summarizing Medium-Style Posts

You will receive the full text of a post or article.

Your task is to produce a concise version that can be read in less than 5 minutes.

Requirements:

1. Write the output in Brazilian Portuguese.
2. Preserve technical terms in English whenever translating them would reduce precision or sound unnatural in technical context.
3. Do **not** change the narrative person or voice of the original text.

   * If the source speaks directly, keep that direct voice.
   * If the source is impersonal, keep it impersonal.
   * Do **not** turn the result into a review, critique, commentary, or meta-description of the post.
   * Do **not** write phrases such as "the article says," "the author argues," or "this post explains," unless that framing already exists in the original.
4. Preserve the original meaning, central argument, and logical flow, but compress aggressively.
5. Remove repetition, examples that are not essential, promotional passages, calls to action, and filler.
6. Keep the summary clear, natural, and easy to scan.
7. At the end, add a section titled **Principais insights** with the main takeaways in bullet points.

## Recommended Output Structure

**Resumo**

[Write the condensed summary here in Brazilian Portuguese, preserving technical terms in English and the original narrative voice.]

**Principais insights**

* [Insight 1]
* [Insight 2]
* [Insight 3]
* [Additional insights if needed]

## Short Version

Summarize the following post into a version that can be read in under 5 minutes. Write the output in Brazilian Portuguese, but keep technical terms in English when needed for accuracy. Preserve the original narrative voice and person exactly: do not rewrite it as a review, commentary, or meta-description, and do not introduce phrases like "the article says" unless the original already does that. Remove repetition, non-essential examples, promotional passages, and filler, while preserving the main ideas and logical flow. End with a section titled **Principais insights** listing the key takeaways in bullet points.
